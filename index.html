Happy Birthday

Meri zindagi ke pyaaar, Mayur ko
Pehli mulaakaaat

Tumhe dekha jab pehli baar,
Dil ne kaha - yehi hai woh pyaaar,
Aankhon mein teri kho gayi main sooooona,
Bas tujhse shuru hua mera har bahaar.

Sagaai ka woh pal

Haathon mein haath jab tera aaya,
Har vaada dil ne khud se nibhaaya,
Ring se bhi bada tujhe dil diya maine,
Tujhme hi apna poora jahaaan basaaya.

Messages aur calls ki baatein

Har message mein teri khushboo mehekti hai,
Har call pe teri awaaz sukoon barsati hai,
Door rehkar bhi tu itna paas lage,
Jaise ruuh se ruuh baatein karti hai.

Video call pe tera chehra

Screen pe jab muskaan teri khilti hai,
Har thakaan meri wahin pighal jaati hai,
Faasle chahe jitne bhi hon darmiyaan,
Teri ek jhalak dil ko mehfil bana jaati hai.

Aaj, birthday pe milna

Aaj gale lagkar tujhe jee bhar jeena hai,
Birthday ki har khushi sang tere baantni hai,
Har saans, har pal tere saath beete bas,
Itni si baat tujhse aaj kehni hai...

Sirf tere kaano ke liye 🙈

Aaj mile toh waqt bhi ruk sa jaaye,
Teri nazrein mujhe kuch aisa keh jaayein,
Chandni ho halki, tu mere paas ho,
Aur do dil ek dhadkan mein dhal jaayein.

Baaki jo kehna hai... woh aaj milkar, kaan mein bataaungi 😘

Meri sabse pyaari dua
